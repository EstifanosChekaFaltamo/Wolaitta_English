Korssiya	Qonccissuwa	

Ha korssee Wolayttatto doonaa koyro digire xekkan luxiyageetussi giigidaaga gidishin, giigiyo gaasoykka luxiyageetussi cogaassi(meediyassi) xaafiyo hiillaa haarissanawunne hegaa gaasuwan gaytotettaa bilaa, erissuwa wogaa, deretettaa cogatussi xaafiyo seeraa luxissanaassa. 
Hegaa bollikka ha corssee bari giddon xaafettida, odettidanne sitta gaytotettan erissuwassi coraa meetiyassinne dabbotaassi shiiqiya coga naqaashatu xaafuwassi baaso gidiya kiitata woykko seerata oyqqanaadan oosettis. Haray qassi, deretettaa giddooninne lumegezuwan cogay kaa’ana danddayiyo beeraanne nu galla galla de’uwa bolli aappe denddidaagan gakkana danddayiya goqota (go”atanne qohota) /impacts/, qassikka cogaassi haniya xuufiya giigissiyo meeziya eranchchata shoobbidi woommiyo meeziyakka aassiyo ogettanne Cogaassi xaafiyo naqaashshata woommiyo ogetakka xaaxi waaxidi oyqqanaadan oosettiis.  
Koshshiyaba gidikko, cogaa eranchchatanne wodala-ganjjeta anttissidi shoobbidi meeziya laamettiyo qaadaa luxetta shaahoy giigissanaadan ootiyogeekka qofettes.  


Luxetta	Hiillata	

Ha moojjule luxiyageeta giddoyiya hiillan luxissanawu qofetid giggis. Luxissiyo kifileekka dumma dumma luxeta koshshay de’iyo naata qofa giddo gelissiddaagaa gidana beesees. Dumma koshshaa giyogaa daro asay bollaa pacaara de’iya asa xallaa xeelliyogaadan oottidi balan birshshikkonne dumma koshshi baynna asi baynnaagaa pilggettay qonccissees. Luxiyageeta giddoyiya luxisso hiillata oosuwan peeshshanawu  go’ettido ogeti: hasheyiya luxissuwa, oosuwan  baasoyda hiillaa, buzo pilggettaanne pirojekite oosuwa, waattanne cita oosuwa.
Yigettaa	Ogeta	

Luxanchchata luxissanawunne yigganawu zaareto/kaaleto yigettaa hiillata go’etteettes. Ha hiillati xaafetta sima paaceta, shiishotanne wurssetta paacetu qommo gidanawu danddayoosona. Kaallidi de’iya zaareto yigettaa hiillati loohanchchata yigganawu denddodan maaddoosona.

Congaassi xaafiyogee aybee? Giyaagaa be’anaappe kasetidi conggay aybee giyagaa shaakkidi biyogee gamma. Conggaa (media) giyogee ha”i wodiyan ha nuuni de’iyo lumegezuwan(salo gufanto- sa’a micettan) merettida meetiya qaattaa (communication revolution)nne laamiya giddon naqaashay kaa’iyo beeraa bessiyo ogetuppe issuwanne ayifettiya ogiya giidi ekkanawu danddayettees. Conggaa giya sunttay shiisheretto qaala gididi muuca qaaladan <coraassi naqaashaa gattuwa> giyagaappe xeeraa cakkidi medhdhido ooratta sunttaanne oosuwa eeshshan amppidi <mediya> giyagaassi immido suntta. Ha wodiyan nu de’iyo lumegezoy naqaashshaa gitiya diizzan oyqettidi issisaa shiiqidi issi heera woykko mantte gididoogaappe denddidaagan salo gufanttuwan awa tenqqaaninne hanidabay nu naqaashaa hasettaa abbiyaa keenaa liphida ayfee xeellanaappe biidi dolida awi wullanaashin erettanawu, be’ettanawu woykko siyettanawu danddayiyogee keehin qoncce gidiis. Hegaa gaasuwan asa ubbay, usakka qassi diccida biittay ha meetiya qaattaa dichchaa di’oy meennaadan keehin qanttan gixxidi naqaashshaa giyaa go’ettiiddi de’oosona. Ha zammaana asa bariyan naqaashaa oosoy kattaappenne haattaappe munnuuqiya ginan asaa naatu de’uwaa giddon sohuwa oyqqiiddi de’ees. Ha wodiyan meette olay zammaana olaa miishshatuppe (misaa’eeliya takiloogiyappeekka) keehin dares. Arggo gaxan de’iya biittay arshsho gaxan de’iya biittaa naqaashaa gitiyan gelidi qohanawunne maaddanawu danddayiyogaa, olawu gidin sigawu bari qatuwappe qaaxxennan de’iiddi liphida ayppee zaaranaashin gakkidi allanawu danddayiyogaa be’iiddi de’eettes. Hegaa gishshawu meete comeqay/coraa murutissiya eqotay/ (industry) awugaappenne keehin dariya ooso qaadaa medhdhiya erettida ooso macara gidiiddi de’ees. Ha meette oosuwassi maaddiya dumma dumma ogeta qassi conggata geettes. Eti bantta giddon keehin cora gidishin etappe aypettiyageeti: qalaaxiyaa (dabidaabbiya), odaa (Iraadooniya), odibessaa (televizhiiniya), odoyeechchaa (gaazeexaa) nne deretteettaa canggata (social medias through internet) giidi ekkanawu danddayettees. Hageetuppe kareera qassi galla galla gaytottettawu maaddiya haasayata (giyawu, ogiyawu, lallabawu, yootettuwawu /conversation/, cagawu /silkkiyawu/ cala-oduwawu) nne hhm… gattidi oyqqees. Hegeeta ubbaassikka haniya naqaashshaa a maaran a maaran qizaggidi xaafiyo a qottay qottay de’ees. He qottata naagidi xaafiyogaa qassi cogaassi xaafiyo hiillata giidi ekkeetees.

1.1.1Qalaaxiya koohuwa (Envelop) 
Qalaaxiya koohuwa bolli qoncce naaqaashatinne gamma qottay keehin koshshiyaaba. Hegeetuppe ayfettiyaageeti gamma sunttaanne qatuwa.  
A/ Gamma sunttaa- asaassi bessiyaa bonchchuwa qonccissiyo sunttati saapho qaalaaxiya bolli  uttana bessoosona. Hageetikka manttaawa, manttaayee, giyoogee hara xekka qonccissiya bonchcho sunttati kaallenna asassi geloosona. Wolayttan qassi cora bonchcho sunttati de’oosona. Hegeetuppe amaridaageeti:- Godoo, Daannawu, Godee, Daannee, Abee, Godattee, Aayee, h.h.m. sunttay gasatta asaassi sunttaappe kasetidi yaana danddayees. Hegaappe kareera cimaa,qeesiya, phaaphaasiya, pastteriya, … giya ammano sunttay, hegaadankka dottoriya, injjineeriya, aakimiya, … giya ooso sunttay uttana danddayees. 
B/ Qatuwa :- qatiy awudeenne qalaaxiya koohuwa bollan muuca qaalan xaafettana bessenna. Qoncce hanotan kiittiyaageaanne ekkiyaaga qatoy xaafettidi uttana koshshees. Qatoy naa’aageekka qoncciyan uttana xaykko xaafido qalaaxee ogiyaa balidi hanennasan kundaanawunn xuuraa buuran laalana danddayiyo gishshawu keehi naagettiyogaa koshshees.

1.1.2Qaalaaxiya 
Qalaaxee heezzu kifileta oyqqees. Hegeetikka donttaa(huuphessaa),

A/ Huuphessaa 
Issi zirxxa qallaxiyaassi  huuphessay keehi koshshiya kifiletuppe issuwa gididi asaa somi”uwa mala. Hegee xaafettiyogee ushchcha huuphessa tenqqan gididi ba giddon kiittyaagaa qatuwanne gallassaa gattidi oyqqees. Issito issitoo bari  mata laggiyawu/ miiriyawu qalaaxiya xaafidi qatuwa xaafennan meezedan ekettikkoonne qalaaxxiya xaafiyo seeraa ayfiyan xelliyo wode nashettennaba. Gaasoykka ereesi giido laggee guye zaaridi xaafeettida qalaaxiyawu zaaruwa xaafanawu koyiyode bari laggiya qatuwa dogana danddyiyo hanotay merettiyogaana. Qassikka qalaaxiya eesotettaanna A immiyo zaaruwa wodiya eesotettaa qonccissiyogee ha kifiliyaana gidiyo gishshawu awudenne paccana bessenna. Qatuwa xaafiyo wode zokkisso sunttsti issitoo issitoo muuca qaalan xaafettana danddayoosona. Leem-TTDM,Wolaytta moottan B.Soore Allanaa. 
 Qalaaxiyan koohuwan de’iyaa qatoynne qalaaxiya bollan de’iya qatoy oyqqiyooban issi mala gididi utettaa sohuwa laamettees. Hegeekka qalaaxiya kiittiyaagaa qatoy huuphessa ushachcha tenqqan xaafettiyogaanne ekkiyaagaa qatoy qassi kiittiyaagaa qatuw kaallidi haddirssa tenqqan uttiyogaa.  
Qatuwa  kaallidi sarotay uttees. He sohoy xaafiyo qalaaxiyaassi gelo gidiyo gishshawu keehin puulattanawunne nabbabiyaagaa kaha kaqqanawu, kaalliya sohuwakko esuwan kanttidi issi gubbisuwan wurssanaadan keehippe yeemoyiyaaga gidana bessees. Neen ne xaafiyo miiriyaassi/ laggiyaassi/ dabbuwasssi de’iyaa siiquwa bonchchuwa saaphotettaa qonccissaada shiishshada year ekkiyoogaadan, shiiqada somi;uwa sa’aa gattada ziggiyoogaadan nabbabbiyaagee bari kahan be;anaadan oottiyo sohoy hagaa gidiyogaa erana bessees.  
Doommaydda keehi denttettiya bonchcho/ siiqo suntan doommada hookkuwan shaakkana bessees. Leemi.. taayeenawu, tagodawu, ta shemppuwa godawu, ta siiquwawu, ta shemppuwa godee, ta namisaa ta saamuwa godee, ta siiqee, ta ushchcha ayfee, ta na”ee, …giyaagaa sunttaappee  kaseyidi xaafidi bessiyoogee qalaaxiya keehin puualayees.  Woggayuwa-  sarotan  go’ettiyo sunttatunne eta qonccissya sunqqo sunttatu ubbaa koyro machchallay woggatana bessees.  
Leem.. Siiqo Ishaa,  Manttaawaa, Profeeseriya h.h.m
