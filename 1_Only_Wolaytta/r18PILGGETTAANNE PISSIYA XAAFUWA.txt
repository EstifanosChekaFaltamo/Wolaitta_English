Woshetta 

Washetay issi erettidda wodiya giddon issiba meretta hanotta qonccisanas naqaashsha shiishiyooga woykko ha hanota qotta kase de’iyo hanotatuppe dummayiya meraa geddaraanaw woykko eta  giddon de’iya eeshshaatu gaytotata shaakanaas go’ees.
Hageetikka eti dummatiyo xekkatunne, eti pilggetta aahotetta shaakki eranawu go’ees. Hara xeellan ha pilggetta hiillay keehi aahoo hanottatu  malaatta, deretettatanne programmettaa yigganawunne eta xaaxi waaxo eeshshaa qoncissanawu  go’eettetees. Ha pilggetay naqaashshata  issikuutan shiishanassi, cora deretetta qanttan malaatees; payddo naqaashata  pultisses; qonccisso, zokkisso, paatetta naqaashaata immees, issibassi anogaaso  geetettiyaabatanne pilggetta hanotati appuuto polettidakkonne  bessesinne issi hanotta gassuwan qottay dumatibeena miishshata gishshayees.
Gaytotetta pilggetta (correlation studies) 
Gayttotetta pilggetay hanotatu giddon de’iyaa gitta hassaya demmanas  naa'anne hegappe dariya)giddon deiyaa gayttotettaxeellees. Hegaa giyoogee, leemissusi nuuni mattumanne(atuma woykko macca) luxetta qommuwa dooruwan  gaytotattay de’iyakko, Dannuwa ottiyoo eeshshanne he awu deretetta zeretta shaahuwaara gaytiyakkonne; Woykko issi awu laytta kumetta luxettan peeshshiyooganne I demiyoo marccuwara gaytotettay de’iyakko eranaw  go’etettees. Ha pillggetan, hiilanchchati issi pilggetiya qotta ha qotatu giddon de’iyaa issippetata yigganaawu oosettees. Gayttotetta  pilggeta oosuwasikka ano halchchoy  pilggeta variables  giddon de’yaa gaassonne-ayfiya ( cause-effect) pilgiyooga gidenani  pilggetiyaa qotta eeshastu giddon issi misatiyaabay de’iyaakkonne baynaakko shaakiyoogaa. Ha pilggeta qottu eeshshaa giddon missatiya woykko palqqetta gaytotetay de’anawudanddayees woyikko aybanne  gaytotetay de’enan xayanna danddayee.
Xeela pilggetta (observation)
Issi dola deretetta heeran hanetiyaabata gididi nu  garssaa koshsha dentetiya daro qaaddati de’oosona. Hegaa mala hanotatun nuuni xeella pilggeta oottetees. Xeella pilggetati aybee? Eta sunttay qonccisiyoogaadan xeella pilggetati issi qotta eeshshata woykko eta dumayiya merata nu  kahaani ha eeshsheti  nu heeran polettishshin  xeeliyoogaa giyooga.

4.3 Saynissiyaa pilggetta Eeshshaa

 Tamaarettoo, Saynissee pillggetta ano meratta zuleyanassi dandayeetti?
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Daro asay, saynissian beettida eraa co kese meezziyan demmido eraraa walakkossona. Hegee, eya akeekka, Banttn merettan saynissian beettida eraynne meezziyan beettide eray naa’aykka dumma.Hegaaka etassi de’iya eeshsha dummatettatun qonccees. Ha xuufiyan nuuni xeelliyoobay saynissiyaa pilggetta gidees.
Saynissiya pilggeta merata qonccissiya ichashshu eeshshatide’oosona.Hegetikka hagappe garssara qonccidageeta;
1.	Saynisiya pilggetay muccureta hiillan gigldaagaa gididi ha pilggetay oottetiyo wodiyaa erettida xekkata adhdhdi oosetees. Dumma dumma koshiyaa naqaashata pilggetta oyshata zaaranawu go’ettiyaaga gidiyoogee saynisiya pilggetassi baaso geeratudan ekettees. Ha pilggetan beettiya murutay koyro naggettidaba woykko mule qofetibeena hanota gidana danddayees. Gidopeattin, pilggeta hillanchay naqaashata pattidinne qofata makkissidi tumanchcha muruta demmanas besses.
2.	Saynisse pillggetay naqaasha go’etees.
Naqaashati  saynissee pilggetassi de’o shemppodan qoodetoosona. Nuuni nu pilggetessi naqaasha shiishana xaykkonne issi metuwaas malan co kuuyetta immiyaaba gidikko hegaan beettida murutay saynisse oggiyan qonccissanaas danddayetena.Hegaas, pilggetati ooratta naqaashsha shiishiyooga koshiyo giishshawu pilggeta hillanchati hegaas haniiya naqaashaa shiishiyoogee etappe naggetiyabaa. He, naqaashatakka ayfe zaareto naqaasha shiishiyoo pultotuppe go’etiyoogaan oottanawu danddayetees.
3.	Saynisse pilggetay issibasi baagga (partial)  
Oraotto saynissee pilggetappee demmetiyaa murutati issibassi baggappe attin issibassi kumetta eeshshaa qonccisiyaaba gidenna. Hegekka, pilggetiya ba qottati bessiyo hemetancha mera gaassotetappe denddidagana.Gujjuwankka  hegaa gaassotay kase oottida hillanchan beettida murata zaari gigissiya woykko xoqqissiya murutay hara pilggeta hillanchay ootiyoogappee meretiya oratta qofetu giddon meretiya gaytotetta  gaassuwappe beettees. Hegaassi, pilggetta hiillanchchati  eqi bettiyaa issi tuma demmana maladan naggetokkona . Hegaappe, eti wodiya naagidi gigiyaaba gididi qonccee birshshata issi hanotas demmiyoogaa xaalaalay etappe nagetiyabaa.

Pilggettaa projektiya doomanappe kassiyan, pilggetta halchchoy giiganawu bessees.Daro meezee baynna pilggetta oottiya hiillanchaas hagee darotto  meto ooso gidees. Gidoppe attin, pilggetta halchchuwa giigisiyoogee issi pilggetta projektiyawubayra ooso gidiyo giishawu keehippe loytti giigissanawukoshshees.
Hegekka xana’ettiyaa woykko pilggetiya  metuwaa tumu yohuwa bessiygadan pilgganaashunddumaa  kaallidi giigissiyooga  keehippe koshshees  he pilggetiyo ano qofa era xalaalaa koshsheena 
Pilggetta  ooso buussan pilggetta halchchoy koyro xekka. pilggetta halchchoy issi xana’etiyabawu woykko pilggettiyaabawu ciimmaa halchchotta giigisiyoogaa.Pilggetta halchchuwa giyoogee pilggeetta oossuwa issuwa harappee kaallikaallidi oottiyo maaraa giigissiyoogaa giyoogaa. Hegekka, dumma dumma pilggettassi  koshshiyaa neqaashata  kayottan go’etiyo ogiyan giigissiyoogaa. 
Xaafettida pilggetta helchchuwaa woykko, pilggetiya metuwaa  halchchoy  eran go’ettiyoo ogiyawottiyoogaa. Doometta pilggeetta makkissiyo qofay ciimmaa hayo qofata paattidi qofanawu, pilggettay koyiyo keenaa baaxxiyaaninne pilggetta zoriyaa hiillanchchaara aahoo palama  gaytiyoogaa. Pilggetta halchchuwaa xaafiyoogawu de’iya go’’ay neeni oottanawuqofiyoobata birshshiyoogaa, hegeeka ayssi he huuphe yohoy pilggetanaawu koyettidakko gaassuwa qonccissiyoogaa, hegee neeni ay ogiyaninne waattada oottanakko bessiyooganne ne naagiyo pilggetta ayfiya woykko murutay awugakko  malaatiyoogaa. Hageetta doomeettappe akeeki oottiyoogee ne pilggettay daro wodiya qohenan poletanaas go’ees.
Pilggetta halchchoy issi bessiya pilggetta xekkata hiillanchay ooso giddo wooxi  gelanaappe kassiyan qofana malaanaadan go’ees. Leemisuwa hagee issi pilggetta dumma dumma qofata zagiyoogaan he pilggetta halchchotu, aahotteta, hiillaa, xaaxi waaxo pilggetta  mera , pilggetta pacca miyaanne hara pilgettassi de’iya dumma eeshshaa bessiya birshshetata eranaas go’ees.Hegaa giishshawu, halchchoy wodiya xuugeenaan  koshshiyaa  oosotta muleraa dentiyoogan pilggettaa hillanchaas go’a immees. Hegaadanikka, halchchoy pillggettaa oosuwas go’ettiyo miishshata  qonccissiyoganinne  kosheenna acuwaappe aashiyoogan hiillanchcha go’ees.
Ha halchchuwas danchchikee gidide marccuwanne dumma dumma miishshata qonccissiyoogee pillggetta hiillaanchcha ooso. Gelloy nabbabiya assas guye/dendo/ naqaashata pilggaanas koyettidi gigidaba mera bessiya pilggetta danchchikke. Gelo xaafuwa doomiyoogan ano pilggetan halchchoy go’iyoobay koyettiyaa  gididi guyye naqaashaa immiyoogan pilggetta metuwas denddo giddeyoogan go’ees. Halchchuwaa makkissiyoogee keehi deexo metodan xeeletiyaba gidid pilggetta mettota waatidi maara kaallidi gigissiyaakko kaallettiyaaba.
 Geluwan xaafanchay ha pilggetta nabbaiyaageti he huuphe yohuwan garssa koshsha dentettanas bessees.Hegaappe  simmidi,pilggetta pilgganaas dentettida metuwa aaho baasuwan wottees, Ha pilggetta oosuwasikka goophphe gidiyaa xaafotakka ziireeyees .Hegaatuwakka, ha kifliyan dummattida  ezzgiya assatakka deemees.

Hegaadanikka,  mata wode pilggetta ha huuphe yohuwan oottida hillanchatikka xaafuwaa geluwan nashetanaas bessees.Hegaappe kaallidi ha’ikka he huphee yohuwan daro oosuwa oottanas bessiyooga ha oottidi de’iyo oosuwaa qonccisiyoogan geluwanpaatanas koshshees.

Ohettida naqashshatu hara lemissoti irra, tohuwaa adussateta woyko geessa gidana danddayees.

3.4. Pilggettaa kandduwa
Ha kifilee pilggettasi bessiya haydda yoohotta qoncisses. Hagekka xaaxi waaxuwan pilggetta oosuwassi  haydda qofa immanasinne aadhdhidi qassi pilggetta hayddawu baaso geeraa immees. Assa bolli pilggetta oottiyaa hiilanchati makissettida pilggetta  haydda lohissuwaa ekkana bessees.
Haydday keehi koshshiyaa pilggetta sohotuppe issuwa gidiyogee cimiyoogee, geela  eeshshaa bessiyoo ginna  hanenna ogiyan go’etiyoogee daroppe pilggetta oosuwa aahoo tobbe gidiis. Hegekka , assa qofa buussaa eranchatu giddon, otta  pilggetta eranchatu, hayddaba citatu, payatteta eranchatanne dumma pilggetta gadden oottiya pilggetta eranchatu giddon aahuwan tobbettees.
 Pilggetta haydday anottettan pilggetta hiilanchatunne eti pilggiyo assatu giddon meretiya gayttotetaba xeelees. Era haydday hara gujjetiyaba de’ishinka pilggettan issoy isuwaa maadiyoo  gaytotteta, oorata pilggetta hiilanchata eran tuchisi dichiyoo gayttoteta. Guugiyaa buqura naqaahshabaappe co yiigisi medhdhiyoogaanne assa oosuwa wuuqidi ootiyoogaa .
Daro assay kandduwaaba qofiyoo wode, eti qofiyoogee issibay gamma woykko balla giidi shaakiyo wogga qofossonaa.Hegeka. leemissuwas, worqqa wogga “Neesi haratti oottanasi ne koyioobata neeni hara assasi ottaa”  Eratteta oosuwan koyetiyaa eeshsha begeka.  Hippokratikka caaquwaa” ubabappe kasiyan, ay qohoka gattoppa” Ammanuwan azazzotta” shemppuwa  woroppa” kayima haasayata hegekka confushiyassa haasaya milatiyaageta.Hagee daro eretidda kandduwa birshetta eketiyaanne eketnaa eeshatta shaakki eriyo eeshsha wogatta.
Daro assati kandduwa luxetta keetaninne  goynno keetan Woyko hara deretteta  heeratuoppe luxossona.Daro assay bantta likkiyanne bala shaakki eriyoo kaha na’ateta wodiyaappe dommidi gidikkonne, suuretteta qofay de’o laytta ubban dicciyaaga assay dumma dumma dichcha xekkata kayimiyo wodiyan ubban kantossoona. kanddoy gede dola hassayatta ge’etetiyo saan ubasan de’oosona.Hara baggara, suure qofay dola hassayappe dummaba gidana xaykko aybissi nu deretettan keehin daro kandduwa palamattinne yohoti de’iyoona? Ha qofan maayetenagan issi purumida qonccissoykka, ubba assaykka amarida kotta kandduwa hassayosonaa . Giddoppeattin, dumma assati ha kandduwa bantassi de’iyaa dola xeelaanne de’uwaa meeziya heregga zixxliyogan kandduwa dumma oganyirggiyoogaa, go’ettosonana geedarossonaa.
Daro derettetatussi  eeshsha ayssiya seeraa wogati de’osoona. Gidikkonne ha suuretteta wogati higgiyappe keehi akkiyoone xaafettidi giigetidaba giddena meray de’ees.Daro deretettati higgiyaa ahottetan ekettida suuretteta kontiyaa mintanawu go’etikonne, suuratteta higgiyaa woggatikka issi mala qofan go’etiygeta gidikokka, suuretata higgee issiuwaa gididi suuratetta gidenan agana danddayees. woykko higgeppe kare hanotta gididi qassi suuretta gidanawu danddayees .Nuuni kandduwa qofata geerattanneseerata boranas, yigganasinne paattanaassi go’etana dandayoos. Aadhdhida 100 layttan hanettidaban, daro deretta laamiya eranchati qofiddo suurettetappe gaxxan tuma piixenna seeraa eqqettana gishshawu he deretteta biittaa assati seeraawu eenno geenadan dentetidosonaa. Sarottetan poletiyaa derettata qofaa menttenagee deretettay kawo  aysuwa lefatteta ogiyan qonccissiyoogaa.
Neeni  pilggetta ottana qofidaba gidiko , pilggetta kanddoy keehippe koshshees.Ay wodenne nuuni assa bolli pilggetta oottiyaabaa gidikko , ha pilggettan hashshetiya assatu payattetta naagiyoogee ubappe aadhiya wuda immetiyo yoho. Darotto pilggetta oyshshay kaalo koshshiyaaba.Haga giyoogee, neesi assa bolli woykko pilggtta oosuwa bolli qohuwaa gatiyaa dooroy immetiyaba gidikko, qohuwaa kontiya toosanasi doorana bessiyobay pilggetta oosuwaa giyooga.

3.4.1. Pilggetta Oosuwan bessiya kanddoy aybee? 
‘Ethics’ giyoo qalay Girikke doanan’ ethos’ giyogappe yiidaba gidishin ayo birshettayka kandduwa giyooga.kandduwa hanottatu eeshaa hiillan pilggiyooga lo’o iitta’ mizza’ ‘bala’ giidi woykko ha eeshatta alaliyaan phoolisi go’etiyoo  bala xaaxi waaxo geerata pilgiyooga.Hegekka, issi kaati bayna hiyeessatetan barchetiyagaa  saynissiyanne deretetta assa guyye olidi aba qofiyooga gidena.
Daroassay kandduwaba qofiyo wode /mizza’ne bala’ eranchcha kandduwa, Amanuwa qofa, kayima hassayatu giddon shaakiyaa kanddotuba qofossonaa.Hegassi daro assay kandduwa eketiyaaka eketennagetuppe shaakiya eeshsha woggatta giidi birshosoona.
kandduwa birshiyoo hara ogeeti eeshaa woykko kanduwaa xekkatta luxxiyaa eratteta baazatta xongoliyoogan .Hegetikka Ootta pilggetta meretanne goynnuwa, seerata, assa qofa buussaba ne deretetta de’uwaa maara luxetta baazaa. kandduwa hiillaa ogiyaa woykko ay ogiyaan kanduwa bessana koshshiykko  maacciyoonne deexxo yohotta woykko mettota paattiyo xeelaa getettidikka birshetees. leemissuwas salo gufantto ho’otteta milatiya xeeri baynna yohotan , issi  assi aqquwaa, dolla heera meretta , kawo ayssuwa woyko haydda xeela ha mettuwayo gaasodan ekana danddayees.
Daro luxxetta baazatuwa , eqqottatu era oosotusi etu alaliyaanne keeruwas issuwaw issuwaw dumma banqatiyaa peshsha / seerati de’oosonaa.Ha seeratikka luxxettana era baaza baratta banu oostta leqqisidi kaaletanasinne deretettay etun amanetiyoo hanotta medhanas kaafosoona . Leemisuwas,kandduwa woggati xalletu giiguwan higgiyaan, enjjineeringiyaanne zal’iyaa oosuwan beetanawu bessiyaa karddotta kaaletosonaa.kandduwawoggati pilggetta  keerottana halchotta  hagaaziyoogan saynissee pilggettaa oottiyaa assasikka, hara eratetanne mrdhdhiyo oosotusi ho’aan pe’ees. Ha dummatida luxetta baaza gidiyanne ha woggatta xanaiyaagee pilggetta kandduwa geetetees.

3.4.2. Pilggetta konddoy aybissi koshshii? 
Pilggetta oossuwan kandduwa woggatta aybssi koshiyaakko  baxxuxan  bessiyo unddenna gaassoti de’oossona. Koyruwan, ha woggati pilggetta allaliyaa xoqqisosona .Hegekka era, tumanne balata xayissiyooga. Lemissuwssi, naqaasha baappe medhdhiyooga, maltiyaa woykko hanena oggiyan pilggetta  naqaashaapaattiyogga tumatteta xoqqissiyogan bala xayssees. 
Naa’antuwan, pillggetta oosoy daro hashshetiyoogaan dumma dumma eray de’iyocora assanna ogottata kaaletiyo billa koshshiyo gishshawu suurette xekka mankayanasi ha oosotu hashettidi oottiyo eeshata hegetikka, issoy issuwa ammanettiyoogaa oyshshatetta, issoy issuwara bonchcetiyooganne lagge wattan zupphe maadoy  meretena ogiyan oottanaw go’ees leemissuasi, daro suuretteta wogati hegetikka maxaafa gigissiyo awatteta naqashsha koxxiyo  pissiya , yara zaareta oosuwa oottiyo wode koshshiya xuuratata wogati guugee buqqura naagiyogasi giigetida gidiidi assa giddon isso issuwara hashshetiyoogekka xoqqisees. 
Daro pilggetta hiilanchati bantta oosuwan sabettanas koyigaate gidiyo gishshawu etta  qofay carshatettan wuugettidi xayana mala koyokonna.

Heezanttoy suuretteta wogappe dariyaageti pilggetta hiilachchati bantta oosuwaa bantta derettetawu tumanchaba oottiyaagaa gidana mala  qonccisosna. .lemissuas, pilggetta mooroo oosuwan,  yiigettetappe dendiyaa ooshshat, assa naatu payatteta naagiyoogaa mehetta( do’atta) layooganne  pilggettasi go’etiyogan kaeottetan marccoy giigtti oosettiyabati ubay deretetassi qoncce gidanadan federaliyaa polisseti naagana maaddiyaa ogiyan poigetees.

Oydantuwa ,pilggetta suuretete wogti ha pilggetta oosuasi dereteta kaafoy mertanadankka go’ees. Deretteaassay ha pilggetta oosuwan amaretidaba gidikone ha oosuwaa muruta oyahisatteta lamu giidi ekidaba gidikko ha oosuwaa  marccuwaa maddanasi guyye gokkonna. 
Wursettan, ha pilggetta suuretteta wogatuppe dariye bagati hara qofanne deretteta qo’a immosona .Hegekka, deretettaba oottiekkiyo hanotta, assa maata, mehetu sarotteta, higgiyaara maayetiyo hanotta, payattetanne ishsalotteta immosona. Suuretetay pilggetani deshikko assa , mehiyaa, tamratettone,  kawotteda bolli keehippe qohuwa medhdhees. Leemissuasi ,issi pilggetta hiilanchay xaliyaa malibe’yo oosuwa baappe co naqaashshata malan gigisidi go’etikko harganchcha boli qohuwa gattanawua woykko ubba woruasika gatees.
Hegadanika, pilggetta oottiyoo higgettane ogetta  maayetidinne  poo’uwaa woykko shempoybata boli oottiyo suure ogiya kaalena hillanchay ba payatteta dafan yegees hara ba yara oosanchatun tamaaretu bollika danuwa gattees.

Naqashshatta pishshiyoode. Muruta, hiilattane ooso ogettane attamissuwa xekka uban amantiyaaga gidana besees.
Naqashshata neeppe madhdoppa , quccoppa woykko wora malatoppa.Ne ooso yaratta, marccuwa immiyaa eqqotta woyko kawotteta cimmoppa.
B.	 Eridi Baliyoobappe Geyiyooga.
Experimentiyaa giigisyogan, naqashsha paattiyo wode, naqaasha gogoliyoode , yarata ha oosuwa zaari be’iyoode, oosanchatta dooriyogan, marccuwa maduwas xaafiyoode, issi oosuwas erancha dooriyooganne hara pilggetta oosuwan xalla eesha koshiyoode uban hashattenne ne qofata zuubarisenaoottana bessees. Qofa luukiyo nena cimiyooga neeppe dentariga.Ne pilggetta bolli hanena qohuwaa gattana dandayiya ne huuphpe mettota giddin koshiya marccuwaa yeemotiyobata ubba neepe digarga ixxikko keehippe giibisaa.
C.	 Tumanchatetta.
Ne gelido hiddootatanne mayetidobatta naaga; tumatetan oottaa, ne qofaynne ne ootiyobati issuwa gidana bessees.
D.	Naagetuwaa 
Azalla balanne sheneho giyooba neeppe xayssa ne oossotanne ne yarath oossotta kehippe naagetiyooganne hiilan makissa . Naqqashsha shiishuwan. Pilggetta giigisuwanne eqqottetarane journallettara hanettiya gaytotetta bessiyanne hara pilggetta oosottu loytta mazagabeda naaga.
E.	Qonccetteta. 
Naqashshata , murutata ,qofata miishshatanne dumma dumma buquaretta harara koxxa.Haratu bori- nashshauwasine ooratta qofatuns qonccegidda
F.	Guugee  Bugura Bonchiyooga. 
Oosotu dumma malaatata, Hara oosoy duugetenadan naagiyaa maatanne hara qommo guugee buqurata bonchcha. Attametibenna naqashshat,  hiilatataizaawu sheniyappe gaxara go’ettoppaa sabana besiyabatus koshshiyadan sobba imma. Ha pilggetta allaliyaasi kaafiyaa  ubatusika kosishiyaa nashshuwa woykko sabba imma.
G.	Xuuraa Naagiyoogaa.
Xuuraa naagiyoo meeteta naaga.Hegekka, attametanawu shiigidda dumma dumma oosottanne marccotta , oossanchatan xaafo naqashshata , zal’iyaa woykko tonddee xuurata ne harganchata xaafo naqaashata.
H.	Awattetan Attamissiyoogaa.
Ne oosotta attamisiyoo wode pilggetta era dichchanawnne leqqidi beetiyoo ossota ottiyoogasi peatin ne kancheeyoora oosotta assanas qofiyoogan attamissoppa co kasegaa zari zari ootiyaanne daro buquratta xayssiyagadan attamissoppa.
I.	Awattetan (Hogogiyooganne) kaafiyooga.
Tamaaratta tamarisiyoogan kaafiyoganne zooriyoogan kaafa etu sarotteta sinayiyoogan etu bantta qofadan maaccanawu ettas sheniyaa imma. 
J.	Issippe ootiyaa yaatta Bonchiyoogaa.
Nenara issippe oottiyaa yaratta bonchiyoogaa issuwa issuwaa bessiyadan naagaa. Woykko issi ayfiyan xeellaa
K.	Derettettaba   Tookiyooga.
Deretteta dichanne barataa gidiyabata sintaya .Hegadanika deretteta bolli gakkiya qohota ne ootiyoo pilggettan de’iyaa tamarisiyooganinne dentetiyoogan teqqa.
L.	Assatteta shaakenan ootiyooga.
Matuman, sheeshan, kochchani woykko etu saynissee leqqattetara minottera gaytotetay baynna gaassota co’oamphiyoogan ne ooso yarattane tamareta eraydda qohiyoonne shaaki xeeluwaa neppe xayssa .
M.	Eranleqqiyooga.
Adussa luxettanine lohuwan eranne oosuwaan qara giddiyooganne leqiyoogan ubbatto nena  poshsha .Neeni saynissiyaa eran leqiyoga sintayiyaa xekkata xoqqissa. 
N.	Woggasi Haaretiyooga.
Koshiya woggata, eqottatune duma dume kawuwaa  polissetta eranne haaretta.
O.	Meedossatta naagiyoogaa.
Meedoossatta pilggettan go’etiyoo wodiyaa etasi bessiya kabbuwaa imam.  Giigetibeenane koshshenna meedoossau mali- be’otta oottoppa.

Pilggettani kashshetiya Assata Qohuwappe Naagiyooga Assa boli pilggetta ootiyoo wode etu boli gakkiyaa qohottanne danottada etu go’aa xoqqissa  Hegekka, assa assumateta banattetanne awu maatata boncha.Naqaashaa immida assas dumma naagiyoga dogoppa.Ha pillggettappe beettida go’iyaaabatta gidin woykko dumma toho gidiyabatta ubba issi ayfiyan xeelaa woyko lagge ootta.

 Qanttan, paatteta issi hanotta dumma dumma ha hanotta qonccisiyan bata danchiketta shaakiyoogaanne kumetta yohotu danchiketu gaytotetta shaaki akeekidi pilgiyooganne yiggiyaapaa koyees

4.4.1 Eeshsha Qonccissiyaa Naqashshata paattiyoga Eeshsha qonccissiyaa naqashshasi dumma dumma meray dees. 
Daro eeshsha qoncccisso Haranahata kabboyiyoo pilggettan, naqashshatu minjjoy dooyaa oyshshatuppt gordda oyshshatuppe, wuddo oyshshatuppe gididi pilggoiyoo  oychi zaarotuppe birshshetida naqashshat ubba shoqomnees Gidikoka, eesha qonccisso naqashshatu  minjjan awugetu mala pultott (hiilata) xalalaa gidosona giyoo zawi bawaa. Hegadan, daro qommo naqashshat  dumma dummaa medhdhigo’etiyoo hiilati daroto go’ah peeidi deiyoogo be’idi de’oos  Hegetika qaaxetiyaa missliyappenne hashsheto hiilat wuddo cittatuppe, maxafatuppene xaafetininjjetida batuppe, dereteta meetee kiitatuppe woyko kawoteta xaafo pultotuppe, policiyaa manunliyaappe , sheefappe nne issi assuman xaafetidabatuppe shiiqetida minjja gidanaw dandayees.
Eeshshaa qonccisiyaa naqashshati eeshsha qonccisso pilgeta sheeshatu xalalappe shiiqetiyageta gidokonaa .Eeesha qonccisso giyoogaa qoodetenaba ubba shiishidi shoqomi oykiyaaga woyko hageti  paidotusi  laametida eeshata gidanaw dandayees, daro  qooda pilggetta oosoti dooyaa washshetta oyshshata, sima zawatiyaa oychi- zaarota , woyko hara meray deiyoo eesha qonccissiyaa naqashata shoqomana dandayees,
Qooda pilggetaa goetiyoo naqashshata  eesha qonccissiyaa pilggeta oosuwaa giigetida naqashshatuppe dummayiyaabati allalee qofata, xinxisso qofatanne tummatetasinee issiba hayanchcha gidiyoogassi imetiyoo hannotatun  dummatees.
